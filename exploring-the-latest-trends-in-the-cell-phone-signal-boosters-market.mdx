## Overview of Cell Phone Signal Boosters



Cell phone signal boosters are devices that amplify weak cellular signals to improve call quality and data speeds. They are particularly useful in areas with poor reception, such as remote locations or buildings with thick walls that block signals.

Signal boosters work by capturing the existing signal outside the building, amplifying it, and then rebroadcasting the enhanced signal inside. This process helps to eliminate dead zones and ensures a more reliable connection.
